Real-Time SIEM-Based Cybersecurity Framework for IoMT Environments

An advanced, 4-layer AI-driven security architecture designed specifically for resource-constrained healthcare networks. Ensuring patient safety through automated threat detection, real-time correlation, and autonomous response.

Explore Domain View Documents

Our Comprehensive Solution

Addressing the unique challenges of healthcare cybersecurity with 4 specialized components.

1. Real-Time SIEM Monitoring

Intelligent alert prioritization and grouping to reduce alert fatigue by 40% and improve response times in IoMT environments.

2. AI Threat Intelligence Engine

Lightweight, CPU-optimized machine learning models with Explainable AI (SHAP/LIME) to detect zero-day threats with high accuracy.

3. Adaptive Incident Correlation

The AICE decision-making hub correlates heterogeneous alerts, assigns severity scores, and drives rapid, contextual decisions.

4. Automated Response System

Real-time device isolation, contextual PHI redaction, and automated rollback mechanisms operating within <30 seconds.
